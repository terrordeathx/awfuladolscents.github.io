Exchanges 
We only replace items if they are defective or damaged. In order to exchange we require customers to email us photos of defects within 5 days of "Delivered" order status. We do not exchange items because of sizing issues. Please read our sizing charts, email us or direct message us before ordering.

If you need to exchange an item, our email is awfuladolescents@gmail.com and to direct message us our Instagram is @awfuladolescents. 

CHARGEBACK POLICY 

Awful Adolscents reserves the right to charge a “chargeback fee” if a chargeback is placed with your credit card company (either intentionally or unintentionally) for any deposit made to your account and before reaching out to us. The chargeback fee will be composed of the “administration fee” of $15-50 (USD) and the fee of $150.00 (USD) to cover all further investigation expenses. In case of pre-arbitration, additional fees may apply from the card processor. This fee will be used to cover all investigative expenses to prove that the deposit was made by you upon receiving the chargeback from our merchant provider.

All fraud including credit card fraud will not be accepted by Flower Brain and as such will be fully investigated and pursued under the law to its fullest extent. Any losses resulting on Awful Adolescents behalf will be fully pursued in a civil lawsuit to claim back any losses incurred covering all business, legal fees, research costs, human resource and loss of income.

 

Shipping

IN STOCK orders are fulfilled within 72 hours/3 days (Monday-Friday) after being placed.

RESTOCK orders are fulfilled within 1-2 weeks (business days). 

PREORDERS take 1-4 weeks production time. Once shipment arrives, orders go out.

** Check product description before ordering to determine the wait time **

ALL orders are shipped via UPS/USPS and take an average of 1-5 days to be delivered depending on your location.

We are NOT responsible for packages damaged, lost or stolen once they are delivered (If this occurs, you will need to contact the shipping company directly.)

**Please only order using the address that you physically reside.** 

Billing & shipping address must match on your order or it will be cancelled.
